A sentence is the basic unit of language. It expresses a complete thought.
It follows the basic grammatical rules of syntax. For example: "Ram is walking."
A complete sentence has at least a subject and a main verb to express a complete thought.

The different types of sentences in English are:
  • Declarative Sentence
  • Imperative Sentence
  • Interrogative Sentence
  • Exclamatory Sentence
